Ski Tips:

  1. Exercise in advance of the ski trip to build your strength
  2. Take an introductory lesson when you arrive
  3. Don't Ski alone
  4. Rent equipment
  5. Dress for the weather

Story:

In March of 2019, my partner and I went skiing with another couple who never skied. It was very exciting! Since we have had a decent amount of experience, I had told the couple to follow the ski tips I posted above- plus more. Unfortunately, they didn't follow the first and foremost tip above, which is EXERCISE to build stronger muscles and endurance. Physical training before skiing builds your strength to avoid excessive soreness and injury. Thankfully, our friends were totally fine- aside from a small fall:
